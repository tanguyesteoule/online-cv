About me

My name is Tanguy Esteoule, I currently work as a Data Scientist in the company meteo*swift, specialized in artificial intelligence in the field of energy transition. My work focuses specifically on wind power forecasting. In particular, I study the consideration of interdependencies and interactions between wind turbines for forecasting.

I did my Ph.D., Adaptive Multi-Agent Systems for Wind Power Forecasting, in the SMAC team at IRIT, computer science laboratory in Toulouse, France.

I am naturally curious and I have always been passionate about music.
